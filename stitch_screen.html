My Research Statement

“

'Sarva Saha' — संस्कृत: A harmonious, organic equilibrium and co-existence between humanity, green policies, and our biospheric boundaries.

My intellectual journey is rooted in the critical intersections of Political Science and Environmental Governance. As a first-generation scholar, I navigate the complexities of power dynamics that dictate how our natural world is managed and preserved.

My work seeks to bridge the gap between abstract green policies and the lived realities of communities navigating the Anthropocene. I believe in intellectualism that remains in harmony with nature, fostering resilience through informed, ethical governance.
